# Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard

> Interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze retail sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

---

## Overview

This project focuses on transforming raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive Power BI dashboards.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify sales trends, evaluate profitability, and make data-driven decisions through visual analytics.

---

## Business Problem

Retail organizations generate large volumes of transactional data every day. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to:

* Track sales performance
* Monitor profitability
* Identify top-performing products
* Understand customer behavior
* Optimize business operations

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing a centralized reporting solution for business decision-making.

## Key Insights

### Sales Performance

* Total revenue exceeded **$725K**
* California generated the highest sales
* Washington emerged as the second strongest market

### Product Analysis

* Chairs generated the highest sales revenue
* Phones ranked among the best-performing categories
* Revenue concentration exists among a small number of products

### Profitability Analysis

* Copiers generated the highest profits
* Accessories and Binders delivered strong margins
* High sales volume does not always indicate high profitability

### Operational Analysis

* Delivery performance impacts customer satisfaction
* Operational efficiency directly affects business outcomes
